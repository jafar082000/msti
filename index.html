INTRODUCTION

Recognizing that Science and Technology are the veritable mechanism/catalysts for rapid socio-economic development of State/nations, as well as view of the perceived benefits interms of scientific and tecnological advancement ant the national global scheme thing, particularly in ICT and Science, technology and innovation, the Kano State Government conceived the idea of establishing the Ministry of Science and Technology which was actualized in January, 2009.

Mandate

The Ministry of Science and Technology was dedicated to the following function:
  1. To promote the formulation and implementation of policies in Science and Technology in Kano State.
  2. To create and popularize public awareness and support for Science and Technology as tools for development.
  3. To promete and harnesss Scientific and Technological research as well as their practical application for the State's socio-economic advancement
  4. To encourage and support initiatives for innovation in the creation of new products and techniques espicially in arrears such as agriculture, industry, science and technology, education, health, environment, construction, etc.
  5. To promote, support and sustain the spread of ICT knowledge and skills through the state.
  6. Particularly among the work force, youth and schools to establish, promote and maintain mutual and lastin synergy/linkages with appropriate state, federal and international agencies in Science and Technology
  7. To harness nad nurture potentialities for local techonologies as vehicles for socio-economic development.

VISION: 
To be came a leading state in scientific and technological innovations and breakthrough with a view to bringing rapid socio-economic development to our populace.

MISSION

Our mission as a Ministry is to chart the course of Science and Technology Development of the State though: 
  1. Planned and guided Research and Development (R&D) in all areas here th state has clearly defined comparative advantages in human as well as material recourse.
  2. Keeping abreast with latest progress in Resaerch and development (R&D) in Science and Technology among the industrial and educational communities within the state, the country the sub region and overseas and guiding the course of the aggregate (R&D) output to ensure that Kano State makes maximum benefits of S&T within and outside our border;
  3. Ensuring that various R&D efforts of Science and Technology establishments to conform with the overall development plan of the Ministry and areas always ahead of the current development level of the state in order to secure demands on S&T products and expertise;
  4. Linkage of reseachers and their establishment with entrepreneurs for commercialization of successful reasearch results'
  5. Using the facilities of ICT park to nurture both on-going and new projects, Ensuring pertnership between the Ministry and cognate organizations both in the public and private sectors;
  6. Promotion of projects that is stretegic to the course of aggregate state development though direct intervention with relevent public or private bodies and organization.
  7. Advising government on S&T policy matters and implementations
